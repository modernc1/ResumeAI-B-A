Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 12, 2026

Thank you for choosing ResumeAI B. & A.. Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how ResumeAI B. & A. ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Data

While using our app, we may ask you to provide certain personally identifiable information that can be used to contact or identify you, including:

  • Email address
  • First name and last name
  • Resume data (work history, education, skills, etc.)

1.2 Usage Data

We collect information that your device sends whenever you use our app, which may include:

  • Device ID and type
  • Operating system version
  • App usage statistics and performance logs

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the collected data for various purposes:

  • Resume History (Core Feature): We collect and store your resume data specifically to provide you with the "Resume History" feature, allowing you to access, edit, and download your previous resumes at any time. We do not use this resume content for any other purpose.
  • AI Analysis: To process your resume content and provide intelligent refactoring suggestions.
  • Subscription Management: To process payments and manage premium features via RevenueCat.
  • To provide customer support: And respond to your requests.
  • To display advertisements: Using Google AdMob to provide free features.

3. Third-Party Service Providers

We employ third-party companies and individuals to facilitate our Service. These third parties have access to your Personal Data only to perform these tasks on our behalf and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose.

  • Supabase: We use Supabase for authentication and secure database storage.
  • RevenueCat: We use RevenueCat to process in-app purchases and manage subscriptions.
  • Google AdMob: We use Google AdMob to show advertisements. AdMob may collect data for personalized advertising.
  • AI Providers: Your resume text is sent to secure AI processing units (specifically Mistral AI) to generate suggestions. This data is processed transiently and is governed by Mistral AI's security and privacy protocols.

4. Data Retention & Deletion

  • Retention: We retain your Personal Data only for as long as is necessary for the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy. Resume data is stored in Supabase until you choose to delete a specific resume or your entire account.
  • Account Deletion: You can delete your account and all associated data at any time through the "Delete Account" button in the app drawer. Once your request is confirmed, your resume history and personal details are immediately and permanently removed from our databases. Deletion is instantaneous and non-reversible.

5. Your Data Protection Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • The right to access, update, or delete the information we have on you.
  • The right of rectification.
  • The right to object or restrict processing.
  • The right to data portability.

6. Children's Privacy

Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from anyone under the age of 13.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
